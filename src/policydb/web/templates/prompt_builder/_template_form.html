The LLM receives: System Prompt → data block → Closing Instruction. Write your system prompt to set the role and tone. Write your closing instruction to specify what to produce and how to structure it. Reference the fields below in your closing instruction so the LLM knows what to look for.

client — primary or related
Name, CN number, FEIN, industry, business description, address, website, account executive, broker fee, renewal month, preferred contact method. Strategy: account priorities, renewal strategy, growth opportunities, relationship risk, service model. Client scratchpad. Location scratchpads (one per project/site). Recent saved notes.
policy / renewal — primary
Policy UID, line of business, carrier, policy number, first named insured, effective/expiration dates, premium, prior premium, rate change %, limit, deductible, coverage form, layer position. Exposure block (linked locations + amounts). Policy scratchpad. Location scratchpad (linked site). Recent saved notes. Policy contacts (name, role, email). renewal adds: days to renewal, urgency flag, renewal timeline milestones, milestone checklist.
issue — primary
Issue UID, subject, details, status, severity, SLA days, due date, days open, resolution type/notes, root cause. Activity history (dated entries with notes + email from/to/snippet). Issue checklist ([ ] / [x] items). Issue scratchpad. Linked policies.
issues — related
Summary (default): one-liner per open issue — UID, subject, status, severity, due date. Closed issues listed briefly. Full depth: full issue detail per open issue (same as issue primary, above) — includes activity history, checklist, and scratchpad.
follow_ups — related
Pending follow-up date, subject, status (Pending/Done), disposition (e.g. "Awaiting Response", "Client Action Required", "Sent Email"), contact person, activity notes/details.
recent_activity_log — related
Last 15 activities — date, activity type, subject, full notes (up to 500 chars), email from/to, email snippet (up to 400 chars), policy UID if applicable. This is where commitments, call notes, and email threads live.
contacts — related
Name, role, email, phone, mobile. Client contacts ordered by primary first. Policy contacts include placement colleagues.
focus_items — related
All open action items bucketed by timing: Overdue, Today, This Week, Waiting on External, Scheduled. Each item includes date, kind (followup/issue/milestone), days overdue, disposition, and contact. Good for "what needs attention right now" sections.
opportunities — related
Prospect/opportunity-flagged policies — type, carrier, premium, target effective date, opportunity status, description, notes.
policies / renewals — related
policies: all active policies — UID, LOB, carrier, premium, expiration, renewal status. renewals: policies within 180-day renewal window with days-to-renewal.
milestones — related
Policy timeline milestones — name, target date, projected date, health (on_track/at_risk/behind), accountability, waiting_on. Plus policy checklist items ([ ] / [x]).
deliverables_due — related
Upcoming deadlines bucketed Overdue / Due This Week / Coming Up. Includes timeline milestones and open RFI bundles with item counts.
pending_emails — related
Follow-ups with email-type dispositions (Awaiting Response, Sent Email, Follow Up Email) — recipient, subject, due date.

Depth: Full = all fields + sub-records. Summary = key fields only (default). Reference = name + status, one line.
